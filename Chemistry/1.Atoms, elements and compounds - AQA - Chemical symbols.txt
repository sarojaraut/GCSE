Chemical symbols

All substances are made from tiny particles called atoms. An atom is the smallest part of an element that can exist.

Atoms of each element are represented by their own chemical symbol. one or two letter and starts with capital
Elements are arranged into groups with similar properties. Groups are numbered from 1 to 7, then 0. metals are on the left of the stepped line, and non-metals are on the right.
